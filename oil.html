European oil and petroleum product deliveries challenge

challenge = "OIL"

Oil deliveries statistics are a key part of the system of energy statistics in the EU and have a long history of data collection. For this indicator, the benchmark will be the official figures on Oil and petroleum product deliveries in each European country.
